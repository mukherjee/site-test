The Department of Career Planning and Development (DCPD) once again is having its annual Job Shadow Program. As a Kennesaw alumnus, we would like you to host one of the Owl Job Shadow Orientation workshops. Volunteering helps keep Kennesaw strong and connected, and you can also connect with former or current students and contribute to our community.

Here are the steps to make it happen:

  • Submit a transient application for Summer 2021 – Final deadline is June 4 - Check the academic calendar! Application must be complete at least 1 week prior to the first week of class for the session you wish to attend.
  • Submit a transient letter from the registrar, department chair, advisor, or other appropriate official from your home institution.
  • Provide Proof of Lawful Presence prior to enrollment to see if you qualify for in-state tuition.
  • Provide Proof of Immunization as required by the University System of Georgia.

Contact us with any questions at 470-KSU-INFO (470-578-4636) or ksuadmit@kennesaw.edu.

We would be excited to welcome you to the nest!
